ERP-Nexus Purchase Module Implementation Plan
Module Scope
Purchase Team manages vendors, materials, purchase orders, goods receipts, vendor bills, inventory visibility, procurement suggestions, reports, and audit logs. Payments are handled only by Owner/Admin.
Core Tables
1. vendors2. raw_materials3. purchase_orders4. purchase_order_items5. goods_receipts6. goods_receipt_items7. vendor_bills8. stock_ledger9. procurement_suggestions10. audit_logs
Workflow
Need Material -> Create Purchase Order -> Receive Material -> Update Inventory -> Upload Vendor Bill -> Notify Owner/Admin
Permissions
Purchase User:- Manage Vendors- Manage Materials- Create/Edit Purchase Orders- Receive Deliveries- Upload Vendor Bills- View InventoryCannot:- Make Payments- Approve Payments- View Financial ReportsAdmin/Owner:- Full Access- Vendor Bill Review- Payment Approval and Processing
